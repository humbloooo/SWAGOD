# SWAGOD // WALLPAPER_GUIDE

To maintain the premium, high-end aesthetic of the SWAGOD platform, please follow these guidelines when uploading or selecting hero wallpapers.

## Pre-Bundled Wallpapers

We have included 5 high-resolution backgrounds in `/assets/wallpapers/`:

- `industrial-dark.png`: Dark metallic textures for a noir look.
- `cyan_neon.png`: Cyberpunk-inspired with subtle neon highlights.
- `minimal-white.png`: Clean, high-key white leather/fabric aesthetic.
- `brutalist-concrete.png`: Raw urban architectural feel.
- `liquid-metal.png`: Sleek, reflective chrome textures.

## Resolution & Proportions

- **Recommended Resolution**: 2560 x 1440 px (QHD) or 1920 x 1080 px (FHD).
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or 21:9 for ultrawide compatibility.
- **File Format**: `.webp` (preferred for performance) or `.jpg` / `.png`.

## Aesthetics & Content

- **No Text**: Do not include text in the image itself. The system overlays dynamic text (Slogans, Categories) on top.
- **Lighting**: Favor high-contrast, moody lighting for dark mode, and soft, abstract shadows for light mode.
- **Branding**: Keep it abstract or centered around industrial/streetwear textures rather than busy lifestyle shots.

## Admin Controls

In the **Admin > Settings** area, you can:

1. Select from the **Pre-Bundled** list.
2. Enter a **Cloudinary Link** for dynamic updates.
3. Select **Plain White** or **Plain Black** for a minimalist brand-consistent look.
